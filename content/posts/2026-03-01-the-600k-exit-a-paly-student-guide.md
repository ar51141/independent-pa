In March 2026, PAUSD approved a separation package totaling **$596,802** tied to Superintendent Don Austin’s resignation. The figure quickly became one of the most discussed district financial decisions of the semester.

Public reporting describes the package as including salary continuation and health-benefit obligations over an extended period. That structure matters because it explains why the total appears large in a single headline while still representing multiple components.

For students, the immediate takeaway is not only the number itself but the decision sequence that follows it. Leadership transitions can affect timeline clarity around district priorities, budget pacing, and communication cadence.

The next practical step is tracking official district updates: board meeting agendas, budget checkpoints, and leadership transition announcements. Those milestones are where the story shifts from headline to real outcomes.
