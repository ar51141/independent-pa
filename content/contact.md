For tips, corrections, and story ideas:

- **General:** newsroom@independentpa.org
- **Corrections:** corrections@independentpa.org
- **Partnerships:** partnerships@independentpa.org

## Tip policy

- Anonymous tips are accepted
- We verify independently before publication
- We do not publish unverified claims

## Corrections policy

If we get something wrong, we correct it transparently and timestamp the update.
