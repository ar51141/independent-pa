The Independent PA is a visual-first local newsroom for students in Palo Alto.

We publish short, factual, source-cited reporting designed for fast reading without losing journalistic standards.

## Editorial approach

- Report what happened clearly
- Show what is confirmed and what is still developing
- Cite sources directly
- Separate reporting from opinion

## Coverage priorities

- Schools and district decisions
- Student life and public safety
- City policy that affects daily student experience
- Cost, access, and equity stories
