## Editorial standards

- Accuracy first
- Multiple sources where possible
- Clear attribution and links
- Distinguish news, analysis, and opinion

## Image policy

Every published story must include:
- a relevant image
- image credit
- image source link or ownership note

## AI disclosure

AI may be used for drafting or formatting support. Final factual checks and publication decisions are human-reviewed.

## Community guidelines

We do not permit harassment, hate speech, or doxxing in any public comment channel.
